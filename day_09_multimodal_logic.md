# Day 9: Multimodal Inputs (Image to Logic)

### **Objective**
To master "Visual Prompting"—using images, screenshots, or documents as the primary context for AI reasoning and data extraction.

### **The Workflow**
Instead of manually typing out data from a sports card or a real estate flyer, you upload the image and provide a "Role-Based" prompt to analyze it.

### **The "Vision" Prompt Architecture**
1. **Upload Image.**
2. **Assign Role:** "Act as a professional [Appraiser/Contractor/Data Entry Clerk]."
3. **Task:** "Extract all key data points from this image and format them into a clean Markdown table."
4. **Constraint:** "If a value is blurry or unclear, mark it as [NEEDS VERIFICATION]."

### **Why it Works**
This bridges the gap between the "Tactile World" and the "Digital World." It’s how you quickly digitize handwritten notes, analyze the condition of a trading card (corners/edges), or interpret complex architectural blueprints.

### **Implementation Task**
1. Take a screenshot of a technical document or a clear photo of a physical item.
2. Ask the AI: "Based on this image, what are the three most critical points I need to address? Provide your reasoning step-by-step."
